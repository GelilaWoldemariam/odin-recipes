Description

This is a classic homemade lasagna recipe with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)
  1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water. Let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, olive oil, salt, and sugar to the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes, then place it in a greased bowl. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out the dough into a circle and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  6. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, then sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
