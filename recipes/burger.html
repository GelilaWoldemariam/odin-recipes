This is a classic homemade burger recipe with a juicy beef patty, fresh vegetables, and a soft bun. Perfect for a quick dinner or a weekend barbecue!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the patties for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the burgers: Place a patty on the bottom half of each bun. Top with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and your choice of condiments. Cover with the top half of the bun.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
