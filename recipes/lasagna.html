Description

This is a classic homemade lasagna recipe with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese and egg.
  5. Layer the lasagna: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 4 noodles over the sauce. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then top with 1 cup of mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce and mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Bake for 25 minutes, or until bubbly and golden.
